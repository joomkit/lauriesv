About me

Laurie Macdonald

BACP, UKCP accredited 
Psychotherapist & Supervisor

I am a highly skilled Contemporary Psychotherapist, counsellor and supervisor with Nearly 30 years experience in the NHS and private sector. I work in an integrated manner using brief, outcome-orientated approaches. 

I am committed to helping my clients gain a greater knowledge and understanding of themselves and so enable them to make the positive life changes they want and have greater skills at managing lifeâ€™s challenges. Over the years I have worked with the majority of mental health disorders. I have significant experience in working with anxiety, including PTSD, depresion, bereavement and physical health issues. I am particularly skilled in relationship issues; working with individuals, adolescents, couples, work-place groups and whole organisations. I like to make the therapeutic/learning experience enjoyable, efficient and effective for my clients. 

I have a wealth of experience in mental health care, this means I am effective in working with the majority of issues people bring to therapy. Please see my approaches page for a more detailed description of how I work. I am a registered Contemporary Psychotherapist with the United Kingdom Council for Psychotherapy. I am also an accredited Counsellor with the British Association of Counsellors and Psychotherapists. In addition I am a Humanistic and Integrative supervisor for groups and individual therapists.
